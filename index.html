General Information


This website provides data on most pick up legends in the game, as well as overall ranked standing. The idea is to offer players an easy way to understand what pros think and love about certain legends, and why their specific kits give them the best results. Most picked legends are showcased, and they indicate that pros love mobility the most, as it offers best balance between offensive and defensive playstyle.





All the data collected count only the ranked games and only players who part of the ranking system.
